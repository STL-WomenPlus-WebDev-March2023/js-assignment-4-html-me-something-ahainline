May 2020

Shortly after announcements closing both of our offices and extensions of the work from home orders, we decide to adopt a kitten.

I found a tabby calico mix on petfinder who we adopt from Belleville Area Humane Society and name Pixel. She quickly made herself at home.

Remainder of 2020

Pixel grows and loves spending time with us in the office, "helping" with work and enjoying the view out of the window, occassionally barking at the local wildlife.
